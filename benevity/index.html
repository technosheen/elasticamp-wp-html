Please enter your name and Elastic email address by November 15th to sign up for your Benevity reward. You will receive an email from Benevity by Giving Tuesday (29-Nov) confirming your reward is ready to donate.

How does the Benevity Reward work?

Each eligible Elastician has the additional option of signing up to receive $50 USD of Benevity rewards to donate to the organization of their choice. This is a corporate reward, no donation match is provided (including the 200% Giving Tuesday match).

 

  • If you select this option, you will receive an email from Benevity by Giving Tuesday (Nov 29) notifying you that your reward is available to use on nonprofits in Benevity. 

 

  • You have until December 31st, 2022 to utilize this before it expires.

     

  • You must submit your information no later than November 15th to receive your reward.

 

Some important reminders:

 

You must be a full-time, Regular employee or Regular contractor to participate in this. Details on Elastic Cares programs and who qualifies for these benefits can be found on the Wiki. 

 

You need to have a Benevity account to be eligible to receive the reward and utilize it on our giving platform. To log in visit the OKTA page and click on the Benevity tile. Please take a couple of minutes to set up a profile with your information (before Nov 15th) and then you will be taken to the giving dashboard. 

 

I have a question about the Benevity rewards, where can I get answers?

The Elastic Cares is happy to answer any other questions, please reach out on elasticares@elastic.co.
